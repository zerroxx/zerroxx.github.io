Current project

 Over the past few years I have been designing and developing a racing game in my spare time.
I combined my passion for history and racing games into designing a concept set in the 1930s. Aside from the lack of games set in this era, especially car games, I find this era to be fascinating. Car models started to form the shape we know them as today, and the combustion engine we know today, came into flourish in this era.
There was a lot of racing, arguably the birth of nascar happened in the 30's, yet this era is under explored when it comes to games.

The game is set to be a simcade racing game, with the emphasis on building and combining cars, and giving the player freedom to build and race with whatever he pleases.
